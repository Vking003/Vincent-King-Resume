Vincent King

Musical Innovator

I'm a young Entrepreneour with one simple mission. To spread joy with my projects. I'm normally known as a guy who says something that everyone else would think is a joke, except I'll make it a reality. That's how I got started making themed albums with me covering various songs. Despite having no professional vocal experience, or any experience at all, I've recieved stellar reviews on my first ever album release "Black Christmas: A Royal Rendition" which helped to spread widespread joy during the holiday season. After its release I went silent with no new project announcements for a year but now I'm here to announce not one but three new projects. "Love with a V" a love song album, "The V Boys" a boyband album, and the highly anticipated "Black Christmas 2" that has yet to have a finalized title. All are soon to be in production. That's not all though. For each of these releases I'm also planning on bringing back the "Official Vincent Trading Card Game" with various cards based on each of the albums released to advertise the albums. In the past I had released a limited time "Presidential Deck" to advertise my President's Club and hope to revitalize the game with new additions throughout the year.

Don't be fooled though, just because they all share my name doesn't mean all Vincents are the same. Think of this as the MCU but with just me. I'm calling it the VKU, or the Vincent King Universe, which should open the door for more spinoffs and expansions of the universe later down the line.

These are all planned for releases spaced out through 2020 assuming I can keep myself to my schedule. I don't want to rush my projects and deliver a lackluster experience for any of my albums but 2020 is the plan. As for after that we'll have to see what inspirations I get and how the VKU develops. I'm hoping to dip my toes into original music one day.
